Structured trauma education for recovery, veteran-support, reentry, and peer-support programs.

Capitol Contracts LLC develops the What Really Happened (WRH) project system: non-clinical education that teaches survival patterns, addiction cycles, freeze responses, shame loops, and nervous-system adaptation as predictable mechanisms — not character failures.
