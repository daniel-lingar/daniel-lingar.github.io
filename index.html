The ecosystem connects a memoir, public counter-narrative articles, a 5-week introduction, a 30-session pilot package, and a full 75-session master curriculum for institutional review and implementation planning.

One system, multiple entry points.

WRH is organized so different audiences can enter at the right depth. Everyday readers can begin with the memoir and articles. Organizations can begin with the pilot package or master curriculum.

Human source

The memoir provides the lived-experience foundation and emotional truth behind the larger system.

Public translation

The articles translate shame, freeze, addiction, and survival responses into plain-language counter-narratives.

Institutional structure

The curriculum packages turn the language into repeatable, reviewable, non-clinical education tools.
