## Public Scope Boundary

WRH and related materials are non-clinical psychoeducational resources. They do not replace therapy, medical care, legal advice, crisis services, or licensed professional treatment.

The purpose of this work is to teach language, pattern recognition, system logic, and practical interruption points for survival patterns that are often misunderstood as defiance, weakness, noncompliance, or character failure.
